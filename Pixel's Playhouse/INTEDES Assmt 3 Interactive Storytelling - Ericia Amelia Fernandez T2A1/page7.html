THINK BEFORE YOU SHARE!
  1. AI can mimic real people: Strangers could use your photos, videos, or messages to create fake content that looks just like you. This could be shared online without your consent.
  2. Your digital footprint matters: Even small pieces of info like your hobbies, friends, or school can be used to track you or guess private details.
  3. Identity theft and scams are real: Fake AI-generated content can trick people into giving away passwords, money, or other sensitive info.
  4. Deepfakes and misinformation: AI can create videos or messages that make it seem like someone said or did something they didn’t. This can damage reputations or cause misunderstandings.
  5. Protect yourself:
    • Don’t share personal info with strangers online.
    • Keep photos and messages private.
    • Review your privacy settings often.
    • Report suspicious accounts or content.
    • Talk to a trusted adult if something feels off.
Educational Tip
Escaping is step one. Cleaning up is step two.

If you’ve shared something sensitive, telling a parent or teacher isn’t “getting in trouble” —it’s getting help to lock your digital door.
